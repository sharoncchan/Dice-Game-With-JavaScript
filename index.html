How to play?
  • There are 3 rounds in total.
  • Each turn, a player repeatedly rolls 2 dices until the sum of the rolls exceed 20 or the player ends the turn.
  • The goal of the game is to beat the other player while avoiding going over 20.
  • It will be a draw if both players roll a sum of above 20.
